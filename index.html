Done Ronin

Help the ronin collect all 13 birthday cakes within 30 seconds to reveal a special celebration screen. Turn on audio and take care with your jumps.
Good luck!

Controls:

  • ← → Arrow keys to move left/right
  • Space bar to jump
Time: 60
Cakes: 0/13
